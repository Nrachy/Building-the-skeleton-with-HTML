School Information

Brief History

This school got promoted to the national level in 2014.
Sironga Girls High School is a school with a rich cultural tradition blended into the academic and co-curricular activities located in Nyamira County. Sironga girls boast of the solid Christian foundation planted by the Seventh Day Adventist since her inception in 1968. The school started primarily to offer education to both girls and boys but the school was later changed to a girl’s boarding school. Since then the school has become a great school in the whole country posting an impressive performance index in 2014 K.C.S.E with a mean score of 9.01. The Sironga girls got nicknamed The Maroon Palace due to their beautiful maroon uniform.

About the school

Sironga Girl’s is an institution of excellence modeling young women into world class leaders and trendsetters.Sironga Girls vision is developing our students wholistically-spiritually,mentally and intellectually. Sironga High School is located in Nyamira County.We are a National Girl’s only school and extremely proud of the exemplary KCSE performance our candidates record year by year. Sironga Girl’s is home to over 1500 students from all over the country and beyond our borders.
